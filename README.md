## 🧠 Project Title

Retail Sales Forecasting & Inventory Optimization System using Machine Learning

## 🎯 Project Vision

In real-world retail businesses, the biggest challenge is not selling products — it is:

❗ “Having the RIGHT product, in the RIGHT quantity, at the RIGHT time.”

This project simulates a real enterprise retail intelligence system that:

✔ Forecasts future sales demand

✔ Optimizes inventory levels

✔ Reduces stockouts and overstock situations

✔ Improves business profitability

Retail companies face 3 major critical issues:

📉 Stockouts → Lost sales & unhappy customers

📦 Overstock → Increased holding cost & waste

❌ Poor forecasting → Inefficient supply chain decisions

## 🔥 Key Business Formulas Implemented
### 📍 Safety Stock
Safety Stock = Z × σ × √Lead Time
### 📍 Reorder Point (ROP)
ROP = Demand during Lead Time + Safety Stock
### 📍 EOQ (Economic Order Quantity)
EOQ = √((2 × Demand × Ordering Cost) / Holding Cost)
